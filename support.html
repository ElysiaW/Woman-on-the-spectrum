Finding support

Where can I find support?


Your school, college or workplace

Find out what help is available at:
School: You can speak to teachers or special educational needs (SENCO) staff
University: You can speak to student support services or Disability Advisory Services
Workplace: You can speak to your manager or human resources (HR)

Local Council

You can ask your council for a needs assessment and they may give you support and financial help.
