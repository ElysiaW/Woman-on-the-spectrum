Stimming and Self-Regulation

What is stimming?

  • Stimming is self-stimulating behaviour. It can help to provide a sense of comfort, calm, or enjoyment.

  • Stimming includes hand-flapping, finger-flicking, rocking, jumping, spinning or twirling and other complex body movements.
